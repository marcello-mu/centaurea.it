Cirigliano.History, stories, people

Events, places, memories, and traditions of the people of Cirigliano

This book is a tribute to Cirigliano, its history, and its community. From the patient and passionate research of Giuseppe Urgo, taken up and completed by his son Dino and his grandson Marcello, an itinerary through the memory of a village takes shape: its places, families, traditions, administrative events, documents, and images that tell its story over time. In an age when small towns face gradual depopulation and collective memory risks fading, these pages set out to preserve and pass on a heritage of history, identity, and belonging.
