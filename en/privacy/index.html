Privacy

This notice explains how Centaurea processes personal data collected through www.centaurea.it.

Data controller

The data controller is Centaurea. For privacy requests, write to centaureaedizioni@gmail.com.

Data we process

The website is static and does not provide user accounts, registration, or restricted areas. We may process:

  • technical browsing data needed to operate and protect the website;
  • data you voluntarily send by writing to the email address listed on the website;
  • analytics data collected through Google Analytics only if you consent to analytics cookies.

Purposes and legal bases

Technical data is processed to make the website available and secure. Email messages are processed to answer requests. Analytics data is processed only on the basis of the user’s consent.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 with measurement ID G-L89HCH0836 only for aggregated statistics about website use. Analytics code loads only after consent. Advertising signals and ad personalization are disabled, and IP anonymization is enabled.

Recipients and providers

The website is published through GitHub Pages. Google Analytics, if accepted, is provided by Google Ireland Limited. Some technical processing may involve transfers of data outside the EU under the terms of the respective providers.

Retention

Data sent by email is retained for the time needed to handle the request. Cookie preferences are stored in the user’s browser until local data is deleted or the choice is changed. Analytics data follows the retention periods configured in Google Analytics.

Rights

You may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction or objection to processing, and you may withdraw analytics consent at any time through the “Cookie preferences” link in the footer. You may also lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority.
